Empowering The Youth

CCYC is committed to creating a brighter future for youth in Africa. We believe that all young people deserve the opportunity to reach their full potential, regardless of their circumstances. That's why we offer a wide range of programs and services that help youth develop their skills, build their confidence, and make a positive impact on their community.

Some of our programs include:

  • After-school tutoring and enrichment programs
  • Mentorship programs
  • Community service opportunities

-What We Offer-

Our Creative Services

Our purpose is to bring our community together and tackle our issues with programs, and activities in the community to create our very own hub and create economic empowering to the community. CCYC is committed to providing young people in Africa with the tools and resources they need to succeed. We offer a variety of programs and services, including

  • Early Childhood Development:

    We provide tutoring, mentoring, and scholarships to help young people stay in school and achieve their academic goals.

  • SMME Support:

    We offer training in a variety of trades, such as plumbing, carpentry, and mechanics. This training helps young people develop the skills they need to get good jobs and support themselves.

  • Youth Development:

    We help young people develop their leadership skills so that they can make a positive impact on their community.

  • Arts and Culture Programs:

    We offer programs in music, dance, and drama. These programs help young people express themselves creatively and build self-confidence.
