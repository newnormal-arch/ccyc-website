About our organization

We are on a mission to empower

CCYC is committed to creating a brighter future for youth in Chiawelo. We believe that all young people deserve the opportunity to reach their full potential, regardless of their circumstances. That's why we offer a wide range of programs and services that help youth develop their skills, build their confidence, and make a positive impact on their community.

    Some of our programs include:

  • After-school tutoring and enrichment programs
  • Leadership development training
  • Job training and placement services
  • Mentorship programs
  • Community service opportunities

We believe that every young person has something to offer their community. We are here to help them discover their talents and make a difference in the world.
